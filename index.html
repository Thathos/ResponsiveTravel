Welcome to the Big Easy

You'll be going to New Orleans! A historic city known for its vibrant nightlife, incredible spicy cuisine, live music, and its constant festrive spirit.

Nightlife

One of the great attractions to New Orleans is that vibrant night life. It's a party no matter what the time of the day is. There's a scene for everyone.

Spicy Cuisine

Speaking of attractions to New Orleans, the food is a must. New Orleans is famous for it's Cajun style and Creole food. Everything from gumbo, to red beans and rice, and po-boys. The food in this city will get your tastebuds singing!

Live Music

Speaking of music, finally we have the music of the city. New Orleans is especially known for its strong association with jazz, and is widely regarded as the birthplace of the genre.
